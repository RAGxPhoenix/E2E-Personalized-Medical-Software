Our website, MediWell, offers a diverse array of features designed to cater
to the varying needs of our users, providing them with convenient access to a
multitude of resources and services tailored to enhance their well-being.

Predict diseases using symptoms

Using our advanced disease prediction model, simply input
your symptoms, and our system will generate potential disease
predictions. It's important to note that these predictions are based
on algorithms and data analysis, and while they can provide valuable insights, they are not definitive diagnoses.

Click where it hurts and get advice

With our interactive body map feature, simply click on the
area of your body that's experiencing discomfort, and receive
tailored advice on how to alleviate it. Whether it's aching
muscles, joint pain, or any other discomfort, our platform provides personalized recommendations to help you
find relief and improve your well-being.

Look up diseases and medicines

Easily access information on diseases and medicines by simply entering
their names. Our platform provides concise paragraphs
explaining the nature of the disease or the usage of the medicine, empowering you with valuable insights
into their characteristics and purposes.

Track Patient History

When you input a patient's information, their medical history is
automatically recorded and stored. You can conveniently access
this history from your profile whenever needed. This feature allows you to keep track of patients' health
records efficiently and ensures easy retrieval for future reference.

Suggest Feedbacks to the Admins

Through the feedback feature, you have the opportunity to propose
changes or offer recommendations directly to the administrators.
This communication channel enables you to share your insights, suggestions, or concerns with the admins
effectively. By utilizing this feature, you can contribute to improving
the platform and enhancing user experience collaboratively.
